An effective long-term treatment option for patients suffering from endometriosis-associated pain.


Bi and Xie, researchers from The Yan’an People’s Hospital, recently published the results of their retrospective study titled “Effect of neuromuscular electrical stimulation for endometriosis-associated pain” in Medicine. The study hoped to elucidate the effects of using a complementary and alternative medicine, namely neuromuscular electrical stimulation (NMES), for the treatment of endometriosis-associated pain (EAP).

Electrical Nerve Stimulation for Arthritis Pain


If you’ve lived with arthritis for any length of time, your doctor has undoubtedly talked you through a menu of pain-relief options. Medicines you rub on your skin or take by mouth, injections, physical therapy, heat, cold, and acupuncture are just a few of the ways to make your arthritis more bearable. Yet you may still be searching for relief. Nerve stimulation – or neuromodulation – is an alternative to medication for treating arthritis pain.
